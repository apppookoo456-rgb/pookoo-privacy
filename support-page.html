Frequently asked questions

How many scans do I get for free?
Guests get 5 scans per day. Signed-in free accounts get 15 scans per day. Pookoo Premium subscribers get unlimited scans.

How do I upgrade to Premium?
Go to your Profile screen and tap "Upgrade to Premium." Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions, and select Pookoo to manage or cancel.

Why didn't my product scan correctly?
Some products may not yet be in our database. Try scanning the barcode again in good lighting, or search by product name instead. We're always expanding our product coverage.

How is the safety score calculated?
Pookoo analyzes ingredients using guidelines from EFSA, IARC, and FDA, combined with nutrition data from Open Food Facts and Open Beauty Facts. You can find more detail in the app under "How We Score" on your Profile screen.
